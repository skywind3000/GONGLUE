BigBoom 和 headOfHorrors 说明：
当你被一大群敌人围攻时，按F10然后输入bigboom，会发生意想不到的情况，有时只会杀死一个敌人，有时会杀死所有敌人。如果你输入了headOfHorrors，你就可以让bigboom按你预期的方法起作用。 

小提示：

Tip: Let Svetlana kill as many vampires as possible in the village of Falkenberg. The ammo you save here will be badly needed when the two of you split up at Castle Gaustadt. 

Tip: Before heading into the town of Redeye, be sure to grab the shovel propped up against the tombstone outside the church. It's actually quite effective against even two zombies at a time, especially if you can back up against a wall or door and limit the angles from which enemies can attack. 

Tip: Several of the game's cinematic scenes are immediately followed by attacks from enemies--be ready to start blasting as soon as any cut scene is finished. 

Tip: Explore every room and area possible, including the bloody remains of zombies, vampires, and monsters. Hidden areas often contain ammo or health power-ups, and enemies sometimes carry keys to unlock doors.
